Pre-Surgery Preparation

  • Get a complete eye examination.
  • Control blood sugar and blood pressure.
  • Avoid heavy meals before surgery.
  • Inform doctor about medications.
  • Use antibiotic drops before surgery.
  • Arrange someone to accompany you.
Note: No makeup, creams, perfumes, or jewelry on surgery day.

Post-Surgery Care for Cataract

  • Wear protective eye shields or sunglasses.
  • Use antibiotic & anti-inflammatory drops.
  • Avoid rubbing or pressing your eye.
  • Limit heavy lifting and exercise.
  • Attend all follow-ups.
Tip: Vision will improve gradually after a few days.

Post-Surgery Care for Glaucoma

  • Take prescribed eye drops regularly.
  • Protect your eye from injury.
  • Restrict physical activities initially.
  • Report severe pain or redness immediately.
  • Keep eye clean and dry.
Important: Consistent care keeps glaucoma controlled.

Post-Surgery Care for Diabetic Retinopathy

  • Manage blood sugar, BP, cholesterol strictly.
  • Use all prescribed eye medications.
  • Avoid heavy lifting and bending over.
  • Protect eye from infection and injury.
  • Attend all retina checkups.
Reminder: Additional treatments may be needed.

General Eye Care Tips

  • Eat foods rich in antioxidants: spinach, carrots, amla.
  • Stay hydrated and get enough sleep.
  • Don't miss any doses of medications.
  • Contact doctor immediately if vision worsens.
